# Chapter 2: History of Artificial Intelligence

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Trace the evolution of Artificial Intelligence from its origins to modern Agentic AI.
- Identify major milestones in AI research.
- Understand the AI winters and resurgence driven by data and computing power.
- Explain how modern Large Language Models emerged.
