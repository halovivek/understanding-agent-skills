# Chapter 6: Large Language Models

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Explain what Large Language Models (LLMs) are.
- Understand tokenization, embeddings, pre-training, and fine-tuning.
- Compare foundation models and open-weight models.
- Describe inference, prompting, and context windows.
- Evaluate enterprise use cases and limitations of LLMs.
