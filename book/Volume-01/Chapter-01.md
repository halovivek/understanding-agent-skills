# Chapter 1: Introduction to Agentic AI

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Define Artificial Intelligence and Agentic AI.
- Explain the evolution from rule-based systems to autonomous agents.
- Understand the characteristics of AI agents.
- Differentiate AI assistants, workflows, and autonomous agents.
- Describe enterprise use cases for Agentic AI.
