# Chapter 4: Deep Learning Fundamentals

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Explain the principles of deep learning.
- Understand artificial neural networks and their components.
- Differentiate CNNs, RNNs, LSTMs, and Transformers.
- Describe the deep learning lifecycle from data preparation to deployment.
- Identify enterprise applications of deep learning.
