# Chapter 9: Agent Design Patterns

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Understand common AI agent design patterns.
- Compare single-agent and multi-agent architectures.
- Design planner, executor, evaluator, and supervisor agents.
- Apply orchestration patterns for enterprise AI systems.
- Select appropriate patterns based on business requirements.
